# PrintGuard Journal

 part 1  - Case Idea & Planning

I wanted to create a case for my Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W. I did not want to buy or use a generic case, thus, I planned its dimensions and shape, and its features such as attachment and cooling.

 part 2 - Initial CAD Design

I created the initial model of the case on the computer. I ensured that the case will have correct dimensions and will fit the Raspberry Pi.

 part 3 - Feature Development

I added posts for mounting, cutouts for ports, holes for airflow and made small corrections that ensure proper fitment and aesthetics after printing.

 part 4 - Lid & Attachment

I designed the lid and its attachment mechanism to the bottom piece of the case. 

I improved the design by fixing issues with the design parameters adjusting their values to make some sections larger and ensuring proper printing with support.

 part 6 - Finalizing the Model

I finalized the design by saving all design files
